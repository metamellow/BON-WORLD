I. Introduction

➤ Overview of $BON
Born of experimentation and organic growth, $BON has become something wonderful and exciting; it is the purest possible memecoin - absolute equality in its initial distribution, 100% of the founding liquidity pool burned forever, with every attempt taken to maximize the longevity and decentralization of the project.

➤ Need for a New Token
But, it remains forever chained and limited by its memecoinian nature - its immutable code can not evolve, its taxless structure can not protect against vampiric bots, its potential to scale is limited by market cycles. For these reasons, a change is coming to Bank of Nowhere… Please welcome to the Bank of Nowhere ecosystem: $BANK!

II. Features

➤ Introducting the $BANK Token
While $BON adheres to a traditional memecoin codebase, $BANK takes after classic governance tokens like $CULT. It will allow BON to become a true defi investment banking cooperative. Both $BON and $BANK will live synergistically side-by-side on both Polygon and Modulus, creating a fluid tokenomic system - $BON will act as a tool for speculative plays, while $BANK will act as the official Bank of Nowhere governance token.

➤ New Exciting Features
-- voting on future BON investments and decisions
-- staking to receive weekly token rewards
-- public treasurey for investments
-- accepted for all BON NFT collections

III. Exchange

➤ Rewarding Current Holders
To reward initial BON investors, a one-way exchange bridge has been created, allowing holders to freely exchange 1 $BON for 1 $BANK. The timeline for this exchange will remain open indefinitely, but is limited to a total of 10.5 million $BANK tokens. All $BON tokens exchanged will be permanently burned, removing them from the circulating supply thus increasing the value per remaining $BON token.

➤ BON>>BANK Exchange Process
1 - Buy $BON on Polygon (through something like Quickswap)
2 - Bridge $BON to Modulus chain (using the official CULTDAO dApp)
3 - Exchange tokens (using the Bank of Nowhere dApp)
4 - Receive $BANK tokens (immediately airdropped into your Modulus wallet)

V. Distribution

➤ Goals of Distribution
Most other governance tokens reserve huge amounts of the supply for the team and key individuals, but $BANK will not do this. Similar to $BON, the entire distribution will be done with one main goal - to decentralize ownership to the highest possible degree.

➤ Breakdown of Distribution
50%
Allocated to the $BON holders through exchange dapp. It is imperative to provide an avenue that rewards early BON investors.

40%
Allocated to the Modulus liquidity pool. The LP tokens allow traders to buy and sell on exchanges like Stoneswap.

5%
Allocated to the CULTDAO treasury as repayment for the proposal seed money generously sent to the BON treasury.

5%
Allocated to BONNFT holders as reward for their faith in the BON ecosystem.
