I. Introduction

➤ Overview of $BON
Born of experimentation and organic growth, $BON has become something wonderful and exciting; it is the purest possible memecoin - absolute equality in its initial distribution, 100% of the founding liquidity pool burned forever, with every attempt other attempt was taken to maximize the longevity and decentralization of the project.

➤ Need for a New Token
But, it remains forever chained and limited by its memecoinian nature - its immutable code can not evolve, its taxless structure can not protect against vampiric bots, its potential to scale is limited by human greed. For these reasons, a change is coming to Bank of Nowhere… Please welcome to the Bank of Nowhere ecosystem: $BANK!

II. Features

➤ Introducting the $BANK Token
While $BON adheres to a traditional memecoin codebase, $BANK takes after classic governance tokens like $CULT. It will allow BON to become a true defi investment banking cooperative. Both $BON and $BANK will live synergistically side-by-side on both Polygon and Modulus, creating a fluid tokenomic system - $BON will act as a tool for speculative plays and community airdrop rewards, while $BANK will act as the official Bank of Nowhere governance token.

➤ New Exciting Features
-- voting on future BON investments and decisions
-- staking to receive weekly token rewards
-- generating a BON treasury through the 0.4% tax
-- use of treasury funds to invest (and buy-back $BON)

III. Exchange

➤ Rewarding Current Holders
To reward initial BON investors, a one-way exchange bridge has been created, allowing holders to freely exchange 1 $BON for 1 $BANK. The timeline for this exchange will remain open indefinitely, but is limited to a total of 10.5 million $BANK tokens. All $BON tokens exchanged will be permanently burned, removing them from the circulating supply thus increasing the value per remaining $BON token.

➤ BON<>BANK Exchange Process
1 - Buy $BON on Polygon (through something like Quickswap)
2 - Bridge $BON to Modulus chain (using the official CULTDAO dApp)
3 - Exchange tokens (using the Bank of Nowhere dApp)
4 - Receive $BANK tokens (immediately airdropped into your Modulus wallet)
