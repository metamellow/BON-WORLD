    • About

    Modulusverse is an exciting and ever-evolving project created by Merp, an active and passionate member of the Cult.dao community. The core objective of this project is to explore and address various aspects of culture using a playful and engaging language, with the aim of informing and educating community members in a fun and interactive way.

    The Modulusverse team responsible for building the project consists of seven highly talented and dedicated professionals. Each member plays a crucial role in the development of this expanding universe. We have experts in character design, frontend development, comic book illustration and coloring, 3D design and animation, scriptwriting, web3 programming, and much more. With this multidisciplinary team, we are committed to continuously improving Modulusverse and providing unique experiences for our users.

    However, our goal extends beyond the creation of this captivating universe. We aspire to transform the Modulusverse website into a comprehensive portal filled with information and news about everything happening in this ever-evolving universe. To achieve this, we plan to include a variety of content, such as informative articles, exclusive interviews, engaging podcasts, and much more. In doing so, we aim to create a vibrant and engaged community, offering a space to share knowledge, exchange ideas, and expand cultural horizons.

    If you are also excited about Modulusverse and wish to contribute to its growth, we offer the opportunity to make donations. Your generosity will be greatly appreciated and will help drive the continuous development of this thrilling project.     So, join us and embark on an adventure through our expanding universe! This is just the beginning, and there is much more to come. Enjoy exploring Modulusverse and immerse yourself in a world full of surprises, learning, and entertainment.
